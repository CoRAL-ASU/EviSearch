The paper states that patients in the GETUG-AFU15 study's ADT plus D arm received a "median number of eight... cycles of D" (page 5, text, "Discrepancies between the GETUG-AFU15 and the CHAARTED studies cannot be explained by treatment exposure because patients received a median number of eight and six cycles of D, respectively."). While this provides the median duration for the docetaxel component (approximately 8 cycles * 21 days/cycle = 168 days, or about 5.5 months), the ADT component of the treatment is continuous, and a combined median duration for the entire regimen is not provided.
