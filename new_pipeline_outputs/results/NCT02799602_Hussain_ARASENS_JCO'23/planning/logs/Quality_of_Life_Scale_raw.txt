*   **extraction_rule**: The study used the "Functional Assessment of Cancer Therapy-Prostate (National Comprehensive Cancer Network) symptom index 17-item questionnaire" (FACT-P), which is a Quality of Life Scale. This is mentioned in the "Study Assessments" section on page 3 (Chunk 8). A specific endpoint derived from this scale, "Time to worsening disease-related symptoms," is reported in Table 2, "Secondary Efficacy End Points for the Subgroups of Patients by Disease Volume and Disease Risk in ARASENS," on page 8 (Chunk 4). For this endpoint, the table provides the median time in months, the number and percentage of patients with an event, and the Hazard Ratio (HR) with 95% Confidence Interval (CI) for various subgroups and for all patients. For example, for "All Patients - Darolutamide (n = 651)", the median time is 19.3 months, 351 (54%) patients had an event, and the HR (95% CI) is 1.04 (0.89 to 1.22).
