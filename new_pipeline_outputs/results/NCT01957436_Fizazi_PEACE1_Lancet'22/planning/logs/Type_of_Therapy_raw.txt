*   **value:** Participants were randomly assigned to one of four groups:
    1.  **Standard of care (SOC):** Androgen deprivation therapy (ADT) alone or with intravenous docetaxel 75 mg/m² once every 3 weeks. ADT involved continuous administration of a gonadotropin-releasing hormone agonist or antagonist, or bilateral orchiectomy. Docetaxel was administered for six cycles (75 mg/m² per cycle, max 150 mg) every 3 weeks.
    2.  **Standard of care plus radiotherapy:** SOC as described above, plus radiotherapy (74 Gy in 37 fractions over 7-8 weeks).
    3.  **Standard of care plus abiraterone:** SOC as described above, plus oral 1000 mg abiraterone once daily (four 250 mg tablets) and oral 5 mg prednisone twice daily.
    4.  **Standard of care plus radiotherapy plus abiraterone:** SOC as described above, plus radiotherapy (74 Gy in 37 fractions over 7-8 weeks), plus oral 1000 mg abiraterone once daily and oral 5 mg prednisone twice daily.
