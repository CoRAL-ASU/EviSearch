1. **TTPSA (mo) | Treatment**
   * **Reported**: No
   * **Explanation**: The specific endpoint "Time To Progression of Serum Androgen (TTPSA)" in months for the treatment arm is not reported in the provided PDF. While the study reports "CRPC-free survival" (Table 2, page 9), which includes a confirmed PSA rise as one of its criteria for progression (Methods, page 3), this is a composite endpoint that also accounts for death from any cause, and thus is not equivalent to TTPSA. Other secondary endpoints related to PSA, such as "PSA response rate" and "prognostic study of serum PSA measured 6-8 months after initiation of systemic therapy," are mentioned in the Methods section (page 3) but their results are stated as "still under investigation at the time this manuscript was submitted and so are not reported here" (Results, page 7).
   * **Looked in**: I examined the "Methods" section (page 3, Chunk 6) for definitions of endpoints, the "Results" section (pages 5-7, Chunk 7) for reported outcomes, and specifically Table 2 (page 9, Chunk 3) which details efficacy outcomes.
