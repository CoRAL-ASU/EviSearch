The "Control Arm - N" is reported for the pooled control group used in the primary efficacy analysis of abiraterone, as well as for the individual arms that constitute this pooled group.

**Control Arm - N**:
*   **589** (for the pooled "SOC without abiraterone groups")
*   **296** (for the "standard of care" arm alone)
*   **293** (for the "standard of care plus radiotherapy" arm)

**WHERE and HOW to extract it**:
*   **Page 1, Text (Findings section)**: "patients assigned to receive abiraterone (n=583) had longer radiographic progression-free survival... than patients who did not receive abiraterone (n=589)."
*   **Page 1, Text (Methods section)**: "1173 patients were enrolled... and assigned to receive standard of care (n=296), standard of care plus radiotherapy (n=293), standard of care plus abiraterone (n=292), or standard of care plus radiotherapy plus abiraterone (n=291)."
*   **Page 5, Figure 1 (Trial profile)**: Shows "296 assigned to SOC" and "293 assigned to SOC plus radiotherapy". It also shows "589 in SOC (with or without radiotherapy) without abiraterone groups".
*   **Page 6, Table 1 (Baseline characteristics)**: Under "Overall population (n=1172)", the column "SOC without abiraterone groups (with or without radiotherapy; n=589)" directly provides this number. The row "Assigned to receive radiotherapy" shows "293 (50%)" for "SOC without abiraterone groups" and "296 (50%)" for "SOC plus abiraterone groups" (this seems to be a typo in the table description, as the text and figure clearly state 296 for SOC and 293 for SOC+radiotherapy, which sum to 589 for the 'without abiraterone' group). Re-checking Table 1, the column header is "SOC without abiraterone groups (with or without radiotherapy; n=589)". The row "Assigned to receive radiotherapy" shows "293 (50%)" for this column, meaning 293 patients in this group received radiotherapy. The remaining 296 (589-293) did not receive radiotherapy, corresponding to the SOC alone arm.
*   **Page 9, Table 2 (Efficacy outcomes)**: The "Patients assessed, n" column for "SOC without abiraterone groups" is 589 for overall survival and radiographic progression-free survival.
