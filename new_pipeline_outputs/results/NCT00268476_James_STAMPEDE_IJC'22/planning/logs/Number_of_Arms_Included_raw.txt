**Number of Arms Included**
Not reported. The paper describes a specific comparison within the STAMPEDE trial, which involved two arms (SOC-alone and SOC + AAP). However, the STAMPEDE trial is described as a "multiarm, multistage platform trial" (Chunk 5, page 2, Abstract) and the CONSORT diagram (Chunk 1, page 5, Figure 1) shows patients allocated to "other treatment groups" (N = 835), indicating more than two arms in the overall trial. The total count of all arms in the entire STAMPEDE trial is not explicitly stated as a numerical value.
